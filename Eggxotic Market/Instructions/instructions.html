Below you can find details on how to play Eggxotic Market as well as its story.

The Story

Your Grandather has sent you to the new local farmer's market to buy some exotic eggs he saw advertised on TV.

He gives you a set amount of money to spend over the course of the 10 days that the market will be open. Each day, you have the chance to view 3 eggs, but only buy one. The price of the eggs can range anywhere from $100 to $900 each.

You can only view one egg at a time, and if you don't like the price of the first egg, you can pass it to view the second. Once you pass an egg, you can never view it again. If you pass over both the first and second eggs, you must buy the third egg regardless of the price.

How To Play

Once you've started a game:

  • View the price of the egg presented.
  • If you like the price, you can tap the "BUY" button to purchase egg, and therefore ending the day and moving onto the next day.
  • If you do not like the price, or you think you can find a cheaper egg, you can tap the "PASS" button to pass over it and view the next egg.
  • Once you pass an egg, you cannot view it or have a chance to buy it again.
  • If you pass the second egg (on the normal amount of 3 eggs) as well as the first, you must purchase the third egg, as you cannot go back. The day is then ended.
  • You lose if you run out of money before buying 10 eggs.
  • If you even want to stop playing and return to the main menu, tap the "QUIT" button.
  • Be sure to be checking for the event on each day, as it may effect the price of the eggs, or the amount of eggs you can view.
  • You can learn more about the current even in game, by tapping the "INFO" button.
  • The next section covers the different events in more detail.

Events

When the market opens on Day 1, it will be Monday in the game. As each day number passes, so will the day of the week.

Events may occur on any days except Mondays and Wednesdays.

The list of the possble events, their rarity, and their descriptions are as follows:

No Event (COMMON):

Nothing special happens.

Good Egg Day (COMMON):

All eggs cost between $100 and $500.

Bad Egg Day (COMMON):

All eggs cost between $500 and $999.

Discount Day (UNCOMMON):

Eggs for $200 or more are reduced in price by $100.

Tax Day (UNCOMMON):

Eggs under $900 are increased in price by $100.

Broken Egg Day (RARE):

The Egg you buy is broken, so you get it for half the price if is $200 or more.

Surplus Day (RARE):

You can view 4 eggs instead of the usual 3 for the day.
